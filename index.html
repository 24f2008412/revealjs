Financial Formula

Present value of free cash flows (discounted cash flow):

\[ \text{PV} = \sum_{t=1}^{N} \frac{FCF_t}{(1 + r)^t} \]

Where \(FCF_t\) = free cash flow at time \(t\) and \(r\) = discount rate.
